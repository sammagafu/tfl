At TFL we care & Prioritize our clients.

TFL Motor Group was established in 1981 at earls court London, UK After several years by 1995 TFL Motor Group grew to a 15 dealer chip set up making it south London largest car dealer ship covering franchises of Suzuki, ford, Peugeot, Renault, Citroen Toyota, Alfa Romeo, fiat, Saab, Nissan, doing car sales service body work and parts.

In 2008 we decide to venture out of uk hence opening our TFL show room in Tanzania. We felt there was a weak market in customer service and after sales and changed the way Tanzania sell cars by offering warranty on used cars and doing services with high standards We currently have qualified staffs that come from United Kingdom to train our staff and update and systems.
