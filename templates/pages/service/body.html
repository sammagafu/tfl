TFL has a strong reputation for producing high-quality paint jobs with professional technical expertise. Cleanliness and accuracy in the preparation stages, investment in quality tools (such as spray guns) and efficiency in the measurement of paint are our utmost priorities.

When it comes to color mixing and fading, we are the best in town. We use a Sikkens color mixing bank with a weighing scale that measures the specific quantity of each color used. More importantly, a spectrometer is used to obtain color and evaluate the exact shade required in painting the car so there is no guesswork involved.
